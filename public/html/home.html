Brock's Games

A collection of game players and solvers built by Brock Britton


Bingo - Record and Call

The first Bingo function is to record a game of bingo, allowing you to have multiple boards and each board will be updated automatically. The second Bingo function is to call a game of bingo, with numbers being called randomly, and the call board also being updated randomly.

Sudoku - Solve

The first available Sudoku function is to solve a board of sudoku. Once you manually enter the numbers into the board, the board will be automatically populated by the answers. Sudoku Play is coming soon.
