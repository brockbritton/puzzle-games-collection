Play & Solve Sudoku

To reset the board or to change to a different game mode, click clear board. This will also end the current mode that you are in.

To play a game of sudoku, select a difficulty and click play game.

To solve a board of sudoku, make sure the board is cleared, and enter the numbers on the board. Once done, choose whether you would like the solved board to animate in order or display instantly and click solve board.
