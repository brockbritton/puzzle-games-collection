Play & Call Bingo


Caller Settings

This bingo caller creates a more efficent process and reduces the redundancy of original bingo callers. To reduce redundancy, use the checkboxes or slider below to choose the columns required to fulfill your games winning pattern.

all


Once you've started a game:

→ To call a number, click the 'call' button or press 'return'.

→ To reset the caller, click the 'new game' button.


More Info

If your bingo game will use every column, toggle the slider to all. If your bingo game will use less than every column, toggle the slider to some and select the appropriate columns. Many bingo callers use a roller cage that allows for a random selection of balls, but this process can be tedious and slow. Especially when playing bingo with a winning pattern that does not use all 5 columns.


​


Instructions

First choose how many bingo boards you would like to have at the same time (up to 6). You may select a highlight color or keep it as the default. Click new game to begin. Once a game has begun, either enter numbers or select them from the table. The boards will automatically track numbers called and if bingo has been achieved on any of the boards.
