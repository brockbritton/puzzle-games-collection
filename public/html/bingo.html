Caller Settings

This bingo caller creates a more effiecent process and reduces the redundancy of original bingo callers. To reduce redundancy, use the checkboxes or slider below to choose the columns required to fulfill your games winning pattern.

If your bingo game will use every column, toggle the slider to all. If your bingo game will use less than every column, toggle the slider to some and select the appropriate columns. Many bingo callers use a roller cage that allows for a random selection of balls, but this process can be tedious and slow. Especially when playing bingo with a winning pattern that does not use all 5 columns.
